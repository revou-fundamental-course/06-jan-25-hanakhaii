About Me

Hello! My name is Hana Khairunnisa, and I'm a Senior High School student at SMKN 1 Depok, located in the vibrant city of Depok, West Java, Indonesia. Currently, I'm majoring in PPLG (Pengembangan Perangkat Lunak dan Gim), where I'm honing my skills in software and game development. I aspire to become a Frontend Developer, blending creativity and technology to craft beautiful and functional user interfaces. My passion lies in mastering HTML, CSS, and a small section of JavaScript, the perfect tools to bring my ideas to life. Beyond coding, I have a soft spot for all things coquette; the charm and aesthetic that inspire elegance and beauty in my creations.
